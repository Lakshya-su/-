序列是指内容连续、有序、可使用下标索引的一类数据容器，列表、元组、字符串均可以视为序列
list=[0,1,2,3,4,5]
result=list[1:4]
print(result) #输出：[1,2,3]
tuple1=(0,1,2,3,4,5,6)
result=tuple1[:]
print(result) #输出：(0,1,2,3,4,5,6)
str="01234567"
result=str[::2]
print(result) #输出：0246
str="01234567"
result=str[::-1]
print(result) #输出：76543210   等同于 将序列反转了
list=[0,1,2,3,4,5,6]
result=list[3:1:-1]
print(result) #输出：[3,2]
tuple1=(0,1,2,3,4,5,6)
result=tuple1[::-2]
print(result) #输出：(6,4,2,0)
